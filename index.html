About Messi

The Boy from Rosario

Born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Argentina, Lionel Andrés Messi defied all odds to become the greatest footballer the world has ever seen. Diagnosed with a growth hormone deficiency at age 10, FC Barcelona offered to pay for his treatment - a decision that would change football forever.

From the moment he stepped onto the Camp Nou pitch, it was clear the world was witnessing something extraordinary. With supernatural ball control, vision that borders on clairvoyance, and a left foot blessed by the gods, Messi has rewritten every record in the beautiful game.
